About us

Who are we?

ROUGH CUT FILMS is a film and video production company and casting agency based in South Africa, with team members in Durban and Cape Town.

The company was founded in 2021 by a award-winning filmmaker Sipho Nuse, who seeks to use his half-a-decade of experience in theatre and the film industry to amplify the voices of Africans through the power of storytelling.

What we do

We shoot short-form films, music videos and documentaries. We also represent talent for film, theatre and voice overs.
