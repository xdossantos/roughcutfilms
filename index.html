Who are we?

ROUGH CUT FILMS is a film and video production company and casting agency based in South Africa, with team members in Durban and Cape Town.

The company was founded in 2021 by a award-winning filmmaker Sipho Nuse, who seeks to use his half-a-decade of experience in theatre and film industry to amplify the voices of Africans the power of storytelling.

What we do

We shoot short-forms films, music videos, documentaries and content. We also manage and represent talent for film, theatre and voice over.
